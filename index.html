About Me

The Chronicles of Riddick

I grew up in a small town on Highway 10 called Markdale. When I was younger, I always had a passion for drawing and colouring and was known for my active imagination. I went to high school down the road in Flesherton, Ontario at Grey Highlands Secondary School.

Within the Yearbook class, I was introduced to programs like Adobe InDesign and Photoshop. I went on to lead the Yearbook team in grade eleven with another grade twelve student and assisted the senior editors in my grade twelve year.

I completed the Advertising and Graphic Design program at Humber College in the spring of 2018. Here, I learned more about the design industry, including many new programs in the Adobe suite such as Illustrator and Dreamweaver.

After completing the program, I finished a 16 week internship at tbk Creative and enrolled in the Interactive Media Design program at Fanshawe College in London where I am currently. In this program, I have learned a lot about web design and video editing while extending my knowledge with programs like After Effects, Cinema 4D, and Visual Stuido Code.

Throughout these last three years, I have developed and honed my skills, not only in using the programs, but also in idea generation, campaign development, and explaining my ideas to others.

When not enrolled in school, I am a competitive powerlifter so you can often find me working out at the local gym. In the summer, I help out the coaches of Sail Georgian Bay, which is a sailing school in Meaford as I am a former coach myself.
